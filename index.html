Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need experience with buggies or firearms?
No. Every activity includes a proper briefing and certified instructors on site. First-timers often have the most fun — beginner's luck is real.
How do I get there from Vancouver / Kelowna / Calgary?
Most guys drive — it's 2.5 hrs from Vancouver, 1 hr from Kelowna, 4 hrs from Calgary via the Coquihalla. We can coordinate a group carpool from Vancouver for an extra fee. Flying into Kamloops is also an option for Calgary folks.
Are there vegetarian food options?
Yes — though we'd strongly encourage you to reconsider. Seriously, let us know at booking and we'll accommodate any dietary needs without question.
How many people will be there?
Maximum 16 men — deliberately small. That's what makes conversations real and relationships meaningful. No crowds, no strangers by Sunday.
What's the refund policy?
$250 deposit holds your spot. Full refund if you cancel 60+ days out. 50% refund between 30–60 days. You can also transfer your spot to someone else at any time — no questions asked.
Is there insurance coverage?
Yes. Full event liability insurance is included. All high-risk activities require a standard participant waiver — we'll send it with your booking confirmation.
Who typically comes to APEX?
Entrepreneurs, founders, executives, and senior leaders from Vancouver, Kelowna, and Calgary — men between 28–50 who are serious about what they do and know when to unplug. The kind of room where every conversation is worth having.
